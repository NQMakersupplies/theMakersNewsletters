theMakers.org has regular weekly meetings every Tuesday & Wednesday, presently from 5:30 PM to 7:30 PM. Our office is behind theSPACE, at TAFE U block, pictured above.

Next week, on Tuesday 23rd of August, 2016, we will be running a special tutorial. This will be facilitated by Kurt from NQ Maker Supplies.

Information on the special tutorial run by Kurt Schoenhoff

Kurt will be giving a lightning talk on controlling some Pixel Leds via the Arduino platform (using an ESP8266 based processor).
 
Following this we will be getting some “hands on” experience with the platform, so bring your laptops if you want to get involved!!!

This is to a part of a broader series of talks and hands on time, in which we will work towards sharing our diverse skill sets as well as working towards a group project that anyone who wishes can take part in. We will be collectively working with a number of technologies including Raspberry Pi’s and transforming some external real time data into information for the Arduino’s.

RSVP by Monday is not essential however would be greatly appreciated!!!

What to Bring:
Laptop – with the Arduino IDE (or try platform.io for the experienced.)
ESP8266 drivers installed in your Arduino IDE.
No laptop?  No IDE ? No worries, you can still join in and work with others.

 If you are just getting started with the Arduino platform you might like to have a read before coming along.
